**Translation (Explanation in simpler Urdu/Hindi, as requested):**

*   **Dil-e-Nadaan Tujhe Hua Kya Hai (دل ناداں تجھے ہوا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* Oh, my innocent heart, what has happened to you? (What's wrong?)
    *   *Explanation:* Ghalib is addressing his own heart, wondering why it's in such a state. "Nadaan" means innocent or foolish, implying that the heart doesn't understand the complexities of love.
*   **Aakhir Is Dard Ki Dawa Kya Hai (آخر اس درد کی دوا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* What is the cure for this pain?
    *   *Explanation:* He's searching for a remedy or solution to the suffering his heart is experiencing.
*   **Hum Hain Mushtaq Aur Woh Bezaar (ہم ہیں مشتاق اور وہ بیزار):**
    *   *Meaning:* I am eager (longing), and she is indifferent (disgusted/annoyed).
    *   *Explanation:* This describes the unrequited love he feels. He's yearning for someone who doesn't reciprocate his feelings. "Mushtaq" means eager or desirous, while "bezaar" means disgusted or uninterested.
*   **Ya Ilahi Yeh Maajra Kya Hai (یا الٰہی یہ ماجرا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* Oh God, what is this situation? What's going on?
    *   *Explanation:* He's expressing his bewilderment at the unfairness of the situation.
*   **Main Bhi Munh Mein Zubaan Rakhta Hoon (میں بھی منہ میں زباں رکھتا ہوں):**
    *   *Meaning:* I too have a tongue in my mouth (I too can speak).
    *   *Explanation:* He means that he has the ability to express himself, to ask questions, to voice his feelings.
*   **Kaash Poocho Ke Mudda'a Kya Hai (کاش پوچھو کہ مدعا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* I wish someone would ask what my intention is. What is my purpose?
    *   *Explanation:* He's wishing that someone would care enough to understand his true feelings and motivations.
*   **Jab Ke Tujh Bin Nahin Koi Maujood (جب کہ تجھ بن نہیں کوئی موجود):**
    *   *Meaning:* When there is no one present except you (God).
    *   *Explanation:* This is a more philosophical verse, suggesting that ultimately, everything is connected to God.
*   **Phir Yeh Hangama Ai Khuda Kya Hai (پھر یہ ہنگامہ اے خدا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* Then what is this commotion, O God?  Why all this chaos?
    *   *Explanation:* If everything is connected to God, then why is there so much chaos and suffering in the world?
*   **Yeh Pari Chehra Log Kaise Hain (یہ پری چہرہ لوگ کیسے ہیں):**
    *   *Meaning:* What are these fairy-faced people like?
    *   *Explanation:*  He is referring to beautiful people (specifically, in this context, often women) and questioning their nature.
*   **Ghamza-o-Ishwa-o-Adaa Kya Hai (غمزہ و عشوہ و ادا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* What is coyness, flirtation, and grace? (What are these qualities/attitudes?)
    *   *Explanation:* He's questioning the allure and charm of these beautiful people, perhaps suggesting they are superficial or deceptive.
*   **Sabza-o-Gul Kahaan Se Aaye Hain (سبزہ و گل کہاں سے آئے ہیں):**
    *   *Meaning:* From where did the greenery and flowers come?
    *   *Explanation:* A general question about the origins of beauty and nature.
*   **Abr Kya Cheez Hai Hawa Kya Hai (ابر کیا چیز ہے ہوا کیا ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* What is a cloud? What is the wind?
    *   *Explanation:* He is exploring the basic elements of nature and questioning their essence.
*   **Bekhudi Be-Sabab Nahin Ghalib (بے خودی بے سبب نہیں غالب):**
    *   *Meaning:* Ghalib, this state of being lost (intoxicated) is not without a reason.
    *   *Explanation:* He acknowledges that his state of being lost in thought and feeling is due to something specific.
*   **Kuchh To Hai Jis Ki Parda-Daari Hai (کچھ تو ہے جس کی پردہ داری ہے):**
    *   *Meaning:* There is something that is being concealed. Something is being hidden.
    *   *Explanation:* There's a deeper reason or secret behind his feelings and his state of mind.
